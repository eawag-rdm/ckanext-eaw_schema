Restriction

You can restrict the visibility of this resource to members of your Organization (usually the research group) or to individual people by selecting "Same organization members" or "Allowed users only", respectively.

Both options are "additive": Select "Same organization members" and add additional people in Allowed Users.

Non-authorized users will be able to request access via email through a web-form.

Access to resources should only be restricted if you are forced by a non-disclosure agreement, by data protection law, or by ethical concerns. In general, all data should be visible inside Eawag (Restriction = "Public").
