Long Term Archive

Indicates that you want earmark this package for the long-term archive. Such data will be stored stored in a way that should ensure the data's usefulness at a time when neither the Eawag Research Data Platform nor the data's creator, her research group, or the current custodians are available anymore.

There should be a department- or group-level policy in place to decide what data should go into the long-term archive.

Setting this flag has ramnifications for the expected quality of the data and the meta-data and restricts the acceptable file-formats for resources in this package.
