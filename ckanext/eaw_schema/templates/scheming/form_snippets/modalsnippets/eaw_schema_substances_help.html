Substances (scientific terms)

List the chemical substances (molecules, ions, elements) that are the subject of this package.

Substances should be denoted by an established name and their InChIKey in parentheses like so:

ethanol (LFQSCWFLJHTTHZ-UHFFFAOYSA-N)

Names and InChIKeys can be found in the PubChem Compound Database.

You can write or copy & paste a list of substances into the text field, which expects one item per line, where "one item" is a name followed by the InChIKey in parentheses.
