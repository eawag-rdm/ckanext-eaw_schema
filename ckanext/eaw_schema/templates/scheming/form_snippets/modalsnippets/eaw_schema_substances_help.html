Substances

List the chemical substance(s) (molecules, ions, elements) that is(are) the subject of this package.

Substances should be denoted by their IUPAC Name and their InChIKey like so:

ethanol (LFQSCWFLJHTTHZ-UHFFFAOYSA-N)

Both identifiers can be found in the PubChem Compound Database.

You can write or copy & paste a list of substances into the text field, which expects one item, i.e. IUPAC name followed by InChIKey in parentheses, per line.
