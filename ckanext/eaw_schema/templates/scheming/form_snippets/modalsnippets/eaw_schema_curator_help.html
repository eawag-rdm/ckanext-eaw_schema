Curator

The Curator is the person responsible for completeness, quality-control and maintenance of meta-data and resources in the package. The curator has an understanding of the scientific background of the data and serves as the primary contact point for content-related questions and issues.

Start typing name or email-address of Eawag user for auto-completion.

Likely candidates for the honorable role of the curator are:

  1. The person uploading the data (you)
  2. A researcher involved in producing the data
  3. The PI of the project
  4. The Datamanager of the research group

  This is a mandatory field.
