Systems

A generally understandable answer to the question:

"Where or in which medium did the measurements or observations take place?"

In many cases this will be

  • environmental compartments or geographical units, e.g. lake, river, soil, glacier,
  • biota, e.g. fish or cell,
  • features of the technosphere, e.g. sewer, WWTP, urban watershed,
  • social systems, e.g. village or metropolitan area, or
  • "lab" as a catchall for labwork where no better term can be found.

Keep in mind that these terms serve to find your data. Use terms that someone might type into a search engine.

You can write or copy & paste a list of terms into the text field, which expects one item per line.
