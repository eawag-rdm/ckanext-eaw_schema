Spatial Extent

The spatial coverage of the data in the package.
Has to be given as GeoJSON - geometry. Possible types are:

  • "Point",
  • "MultiPoint",
  • "LineString",
  • "MultiLineString",
  • "Polygon",
  • "MultiPolygon"
Examples are given at geojson.org

Note that "GeometryCollection", FeatureCollections" and "FeatureObjects" are currently not supported.
