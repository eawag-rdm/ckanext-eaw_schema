Spatial Extent

Entering the spatial extent of your data is currently not yet supported in a user-friendly manner. In case the field "Geographic Name(s)" does not describe the spatial reference of you data precisely enough (for example beacuse your measurements do not pertain to a whole lake but rather to a specific spot in that lake), please make a note (to yourself and the administrator of this platform) in the Notes field about where that information can be found.

In case you want to see your data on the map now you have to enter the spatial coverage of the data in the package as GeoJSON - geometry. Possible types are:

  • "Point",
  • "MultiPoint",
  • "LineString",
  • "MultiLineString",
  • "Polygon",
  • "MultiPolygon"
Examples are given at geojson.org

Note that "GeometryCollection", "FeatureCollections" and "FeatureObjects" are currently not supported.
