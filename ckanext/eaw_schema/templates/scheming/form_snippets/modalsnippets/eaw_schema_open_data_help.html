Open Data

Indicates that this data package should be published available for anyone (also outside Eawag). This should be the norm, unless the package contains material that you are legally not allowed to publish, such as personal data protected by the Federal Act on Data Protection or data that you obtained under a non-disclosure agreement.

Actually, no data will be made available outside Eawag, even if you check this flag, without contacting you again and figuring out the details.

We currently do not have the ability to publish Open Data. The "Open Data" flag mainly helps us to gauge the interest in such a yet to be established service.
